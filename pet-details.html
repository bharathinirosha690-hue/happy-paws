Luna's Bio

Luna was brought into our Downtown Shelter as a stray after she was found wandering in a suburban park. She was slightly underweight but completely healthy. Since then, she has been the absolute highlight of our volunteer walks.

Luna is exceptionally smart, knows basic commands (sit, stay, shake), and has high stamina. She is ideal for an active family with a yard or someone who loves morning runs. She is extremely warm, socialized, and loves to cuddle after a long day of outdoor play.
