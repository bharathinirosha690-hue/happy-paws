Why Adopt from Happy Paws?

When you adopt an animal from Happy Paws, you aren't just getting a pet. You are saving a life and supporting a system dedicated to animal wellness and happiness.

Fully Vet-Checked

Spayed, vaccinated, and microchipped prior to matching.

Personality Profiling

We match pets to your lifestyle and home space carefully.

Post-Adoption Care

Lifetime phone consults and tips to help settling in.

Honest Fees

All application fees directly sponsor other rescues.

Easy 4-Step Adoption Process

How it works: We keep our onboarding transparent and straightforward so you can focus on building links with your new friend.

1
Select Your Pet

Browse through our online listings or visit the shelter site to meet available dogs, cats, or rabbits.

2
Submit Application

Fill out our online adoption application stating your housing status, pets experience, and lifestyle.

3
Meet & Greet

We set up a meet and greet block at the shelter to verify compatibility between you and the pet.

4
Welcome Home

Complete final paperwork, pay the adoption support fee, and bring your brand new companion home!
