Step-by-Step Adoption Guide

Learn about our requirements, housing checks, fees, and read answers to common questions about animal rescue matching.

Our Adoption Timeline

From the moment you pick a potential pet to bringing them home, here is exactly what to expect from us.

Step 1

Find Your Match

Browse through our online database or visit our shelter site. Spend time interacting with different dogs, cats, or rabbits to verify if their size, energy, and temperament align with your lifestyle.

Step 2

Submit Application

Fill out our vetting application form. We require details about your housing agreement (own/rent), references from local veterinarians (if you have other pets), and details about household residents.

Step 3

Coordinators Interview

Our adoption specialists will call you for a brief phone conversation. We confirm landlord permissions, review training expectations, and answer any specific questions about diet, vaccines, or behavior.

Step 4

Meet & Greet & Take Home

Schedule an in-person meeting with your family (and other dogs if you have any). If all goes beautifully, finalize the paperwork, complete the sponsorship adoption fee, and welcome your new member home!

Adoption Requirements

  • Minimum Age Limit

    You must be at least 21 years old and possess a valid government-issued photo ID.

  • Housing Agreement Checks

    If you lease, you must show a copy of your rental contract permitting pets of the specific size and breed.

  • Veterinary Vetting

    Existing animals must be up to date with spaying procedures and primary rabies vaccinations.

  • Commitment to Care

    A willingness to provide appropriate exercise, nutritional balance, vet treatment, and training space.

Documents to Bring Along

Please bring these items to your shelter appointment or attach them during the online application review to expedite approval:

Government ID

Drivers license, passport, or state identity card.

Utility Bill

Proof of residency matching current address details.

Lease Copy

Rental agreement copy specifying pet policies.

Landlord Info

Contact details for housing landlord check verification.

Adoption fees range from $150 to $300 depending on the animal's type, breed group, and age (puppies and kittens have higher fees to help cover primary vaccine sets). The fees cover: spaying/neutering procedure, complete DHPP/FVRCP vaccine sets, rabies shot, microchipping registration, deworming, heartworm testing, and general physical checkup.
