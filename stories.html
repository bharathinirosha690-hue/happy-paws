Luna: From a Park Stray to a Running Buddy

"Luna completed our home! She was found lost in the cold rain and underweight. Happy Paws cured her ear infection, got her vaccinated, and prepared her for adoption. She now spends her days jogging with us in the park and sleeping on a warm rug in our living room. We are eternally grateful!"

The Miller Family
Adopted Luna in January 2024
Before
After Adoption

Oliver: Finding Warmth and Safety Indoors

"Oliver was found trapped in a small cardboard box. He was so terrified of people. Vetting specialists and volunteers at the shelter spent months socializing him. When I brought him home, he purred the entire night. He is the sweetest companion for my apartment work desk."

Sarah Jenkins
Adopted Oliver in August 2025

After Adoption

Bugs: The Energetic Bunny

"Our kids always wanted a bunny, but we had reservations about rabbit grooming and dietary needs. The coordinators gave us detailed handouts, recommended cage dimensions, and suggested leafy diets. Bugs is super friendly and runs around the house like a furry lightning bolt!"

The Thompson Family
Adopted Bugs in December 2025
