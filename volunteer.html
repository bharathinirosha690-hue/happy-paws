Volunteer & Save Lives

Give your time and love. Our shelter relies heavily on community members who feed, walk, foster, and represent our animals.

How You Can Help

Select a role that matches your skills, animal experience, and weekly hours schedule.

Popular
Dog Walker & Socializer

Help rescue dogs burn energy! Take them on morning hikes, play fetch in the yard, and practice basic training cues.

Cat Socializer

Help stray cats adapt to human presence. Brush their coats, play with laser toys, and keep cat tree playrooms neat.

Foster Caregiver

Provide a cozy, temporary home for puppies, kittens, or recovering surgical patients. We supply food and vet clinic costs.

Event Support

Represent Happy Paws! Assist with shelter cleanups, manage weekend adoption booths, and run charity donations.

Happy Paws

Happy Paws is a modern, non-profit rescue shelter and adoption center. Founded in 2018, we work hard to rescue strays, provide emergency veterinary care, and find loving homes for abandoned animals.
