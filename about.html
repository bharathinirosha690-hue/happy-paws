Our Story & Mission

Learn how we started, our core shelter values, and meet the compassionate team dedicated to animal rescue.

Rescue Journey Since 2018

Happy Paws started with a group of four veterinarians and animal welfare volunteers who took in stray cats and dogs in their own basements and garages. As the shelter requirements grew, we opened our first physical sanctuary in 2020.

Today, we operate three regional shelters housing up to 150 dogs, cats, rabbits, and birds. We strive to provide excellent, compassionate shelter support and make matches that last a lifetime.

99% Success Match

We rarely see pets returned because our vetting matches check your home lifestyle.

100% Medical Care

Every single rescue gets immediate vet treatment, surgery, and proper vaccine support.

Our Mission

To rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome stray, abandoned, or injured animals. We are committed to increasing pet adoption awareness, teaching responsible ownership, and ending pet homelessness in our local communities.

Our Vision

A compassionate world where every companion animal is valued, loved, and has a safe home. We envision our shelters as hubs for education, vet health programs, and active volunteer networks.

Milestones of Our Journey

See how we grew from a small volunteer foster circle to one of the region's leading animal welfare shelters.

2018

The Seed is Planted

Founded by four veterinary workers. Rescued and placed 45 stray cats and dogs out of foster homes during the initial 12 months.

2020

First Shelter Site

Purchased our primary facility. Installed comfortable dog kennels, safe cat playrooms, and rabbit habitats, housing up to 60 pets.

2022

Veterinary Expansion

Added a fully functional, low-cost on-site vet clinic to provide surgeries, microchipping, and vaccines for community pet owners.

2025

1,200+ Lives Saved

Expanded to three sites and launched regional foster matching programs, crossing a milestone of over 1,200 successful adoptions.
